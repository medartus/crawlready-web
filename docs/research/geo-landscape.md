# Research: GEO Market Landscape

Raw research findings from April 2026. These are the primary sources used to shape CrawlReady's positioning.

---

## Market Size and Growth

- Gartner projects traditional search engine volume drops **25% by 2026** as users shift to AI answer engines
- **AI-referred sessions grew 527%** year-over-year in H1 2025 (Previsible 2025 AI Traffic Report)
- **BrightEdge:** 752% year-over-year surge in e-commerce referrals from AI chatbots in late 2025
- AI accounts for **60%+ of information retrieval** among heavy users (Q1 2026)
- China's GEO market alone: **USD 3.65B in H1 2025**, 240% YoY increase

## The Traffic Shift Reality Check

- Traditional organic search still drives **48.5% of all website traffic globally** (2026)
- AI-referred traffic drives **~0.15%** — the gap is enormous
- BUT: AI search visitors convert at **4.4x the rate** of traditional organic visitors (Semrush 2025)
- **36% of generative AI users** say they've started replacing traditional search with AI
- **30% of programming-related searches** are now done on ChatGPT (Q1 2025)

## Key GEO Tool Companies

### Profound
- **Funding:** $35M Series B, Sequoia Capital
- **Stage:** Growth
- **Target:** Enterprise, regulated industries (healthcare, pharma, finance, retail)
- **Product:** Brand visibility monitoring in AI answers, white-glove service
- **Recognition:** G2 Winter 2026 AEO Leader; mentioned by Reddit CEO in Q2 2025 earnings call
- **Weakness:** Enterprise-only, expensive, no active optimization

### Peec.ai
- **Funding:** $21M Series A
- **Customers:** 1,500+ marketing teams
- **Notable clients:** Wix, ElevenLabs, Chanel
- **Product:** Daily monitoring across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Google AI Overviews; multi-country/language
- **Recognition:** BusinessCloud 2026 Enterprise AI Visibility Report — top enterprise platform
- **Weakness:** Monitoring only

### Evertune
- **Funding:** $19M
- **Team:** Founded by early The Trade Desk members, 40+ employees, NYC
- **Verticals:** Finance, Retail, Automotive, Pharma, Tech, Travel
- **Product:** GEO platform for brand visibility in AI search
- **Weakness:** Monitoring only, enterprise-focused

### Otterly
- **Positioning:** "Baseline monitoring"
- **Integration:** Only GEO tool inside the Semrush ecosystem
- **Price:** ~$25/mo
- **Strength:** Low cost, Semrush distribution
- **Weakness:** "Good at showing whether you appear, limited in telling you what to do"

### GenOptima
- **Origin:** China, early GEO entrant (2024)
- **Platform:** GENO — covers ChatGPT, Gemini, Grok, Perplexity, Baidu ERNIE, DeepSeek
- **Notes:** Built own 4-module GEO platform; primarily Asia-focused

### BrightEdge
- **Type:** Enterprise SEO platform adding GEO
- **Tagline:** "Optimize once, win everywhere"
- **Product:** Bridges traditional SEO and AI channels, SEO Copilot content suggestions
- **Target:** Large enterprises already using BrightEdge for SEO

## SEO Incumbents

### Semrush
- AI SEO Toolkit: monitors ChatGPT/Google AI Overviews brand mentions
- ContentShake AI: content generation for SEO keywords
- Semrush Copilot: AI SEO recommendations
- Pricing: $99/mo standalone, $199/mo+ bundled
- MCP server launched 2026 (read-only)

### Ahrefs
- Brand Radar: tracks across 6 AI engines
- Pricing: $129/mo base + $398/mo per platform OR $699/mo for all 6
- MCP server launched 2026 (read-only)
- Criticism: expensive, monitoring only

### GenRankEngine
- **Product:** GEO platform for SaaS — monitoring across ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity with displacement reporting and engineering-ready fixes
- **Target:** SaaS products specifically
- **Strength:** SaaS-specific focus, actionable fix recommendations (overlaps CrawlReady's ICP)
- **Weakness:** Limited public information on pricing, team size, or traction

### iGEO
- **Product:** AI visibility tracking across major platforms with prompt builder, AI traffic analytics, and automated workflows
- **Claims:** 3–5x higher conversion from GEO vs SEO
- **Strength:** Comprehensive platform with analytics and workflow automation
- **Weakness:** Broad focus, unclear differentiation from monitoring incumbents

### Clemelopy
- **Product:** "All-in-One GEO Platform" — auditing, schema markup, "Orchard Ecosystem Framework"
- **Stage:** Public beta, launched 2026
- **Pricing:** Starting at $29/mo
- **Strength:** Beginner-friendly, affordable entry point
- **Weakness:** Early stage, limited traction data

**Market velocity note (April 2026):** These three companies were not in the original competitive research. Their appearance in a single search confirms the GEO market is growing rapidly with new entrants arriving monthly. The total competitive field is now 8+ direct competitors plus monitoring incumbents plus npm middleware packages.

## Key Industry Observation

Semrush and Ahrefs launched **read-only MCP servers** in 2026 — agents can pull data but cannot create or optimize content. Neither incumbent is moving to active optimization.

## GEO Agency Landscape

Top agencies (service-side competitors):
- **Profound** (services arm, enterprise)
- **First Page Sage** (B2B/SaaS focus)
- **Genevate** (launched 2025, pure-play GEO/PR, no legacy SEO)
- **Elsner Technologies** (#1 for enterprise by some rankings)
- **Focus Digital** (small business segment)

Agency market signals that buyers exist and are willing to pay — but agencies are a validation vehicle for the platform, not the target customer.

## Conclusion

The GEO SaaS market is **real and growing** but already consolidating around well-funded pure-play monitoring tools, SEO incumbents adding features, and **8+ active optimization startups**. The active optimization mechanic is no longer unaddressed — MachineContext, Mersel AI, HypoText, Prerender.io, DualWeb.AI, GenRankEngine, iGEO, and Clemelopy all ship some form of it. CrawlReady's entry point must be the **free diagnostic with permanent shareable score URLs** — a distribution mechanic no competitor has built — not the optimization layer itself.
