## Our Why: The Problem We Solve

### The AI Search Revolution

The internet is undergoing its biggest shift since mobile. AI-powered search is replacing traditional search engines:

- **ChatGPT:** 800M weekly users, 81% AI chatbot market share
- **Perplexity:** Fastest-growing search alternative
- **Claude, Gemini:** Enterprise AI integration accelerating
- **Zero-Click:** 60% of searches end without visiting external sites
- **Consumer Behavior:** 37% start product research with AI tools

### The JavaScript Invisibility Problem

Here's the critical gap no one is talking about:

| Crawler | Renders JavaScript? | Impact |
|---------|---------------------|--------|
| GPTBot (OpenAI) | **NO** | React/Vue sites invisible to ChatGPT |
| ClaudeBot (Anthropic) | **NO** | Sites invisible to Claude |
| PerplexityBot | **NO** | Sites invisible to Perplexity |
| AppleBot-Extended | **NO** | Sites invisible to Apple Intelligence |
| Googlebot | YES | Only Google renders JS |

**The math is brutal:**
- 98.9% of modern websites use JavaScript frameworks
- Only 31% of AI crawlers can render JavaScript
- Result: Most websites return blank pages to AI crawlers

**This isn't just a technical problem—it's an existential threat:**
1. **Competitive Disadvantage:** Competitors appear in ChatGPT answers, you don't
2. **Lost Revenue:** AI search traffic growing 400% YoY—you're missing out
3. **Market Share Erosion:** Early movers in AI search will dominate categories
4. **Technical Debt:** Rebuilding sites for SSR costs $120K+ and takes 6 months
