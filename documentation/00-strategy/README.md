## Strategic Summary

### The Opportunity

**Problem:** 98.9% of websites use JavaScript frameworks, but only 31% of AI crawlers can render JavaScript. This means most websites are invisible to ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and other AI search platforms.

**Market Size:**
- AI search market: $750B US revenue by 2028
- Dynamic rendering market: ~$950M addressable by 2026
- Market penetration: <3% (massive whitespace)

**Our Position:** CrawlReady is the first AI-first rendering service, solving the "pre-GEO" problem—making content visible to AI crawlers before optimization matters.

### Target Customers

| Priority | Persona | Why | Budget |
|----------|---------|-----|--------|
| **Primary** | Technical Founder/CTO | Implements immediately, has budget authority | $49-499/mo |
| **Secondary** | VP Marketing/Growth | Business urgency, marketing budget | $149-999/mo |
| **Tertiary** | E-commerce Directors | Clearest ROI, urgent pain | $99-499/mo |

### Competitive Differentiation

1. **AI-First Positioning** - Built for ChatGPT/Perplexity/Claude, not Google SEO
2. **Unique Features** - Citation tracking, schema injection, AI crawler analytics
3. **Better Economics** - 46% cheaper than Prerender.io
4. **Developer Experience** - 5-minute setup, comprehensive API
