## GTM Strategy Summary

### Target Market

**Beachhead:** JavaScript SaaS Startups
- Technical founders who understand the problem
- Can implement immediately
- Strong word-of-mouth networks
- Budget authority in small teams

### Go-to-Market Motion

**Product-Led Growth (PLG) with Sales-Assist**

1. **Awareness:** Content marketing, free tools, community
2. **Acquisition:** Free trial (1,000 renders)
3. **Activation:** <5 minute time-to-value
4. **Revenue:** Self-serve upgrade
5. **Expansion:** Usage-based growth + sales-assist for enterprise

### Key Metrics

| Stage | Metric | Target |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Awareness | Website Traffic | 10K/mo |
| Acquisition | Trial Signups | 200/mo |
| Activation | Trial→Active | 40% |
| Revenue | Active→Paid | 12% |
| Expansion | NRR | 110% |

## Launch Timeline

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Weeks 1-4)
- Landing page live
- Free tool: AI Crawler Checker
- Building in public on Twitter
- Collect 500+ waitlist signups

### Phase 2: Soft Launch (Weeks 5-8)
- MVP live for early access
- 50 beta customers
- Iterate based on feedback
- Prepare case studies

### Phase 3: Public Launch (Weeks 9-12)
- Product Hunt launch
- Hacker News post
- Press outreach
- Paid marketing (small test)

### Phase 4: Growth (Month 4+)
- Content engine running
- Community building
- Platform integrations
- Enterprise outreach
