## Brand Voice in Messaging

### DO:
- Use specific metrics ("200ms" not "fast")
- Be technical but accessible
- Show confidence without arrogance
- Lead with customer problems
- Include code examples where relevant

### DON'T:
- Use vague marketing speak
- Make unverifiable claims
- Bash competitors unfairly
- Overpromise results
- Use jargon without explanation

### Tone by Channel

| Channel | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Website | Confident, clear | "Get cited in ChatGPT answers" |
| Twitter | Conversational, insightful | "Your React app returns a blank page to ChatGPT. Here's why..." |
| Documentation | Precise, helpful | "Add this middleware to detect AI crawlers. It takes 30 seconds." |
| Sales | Consultative, value-focused | "Let me show you which AI crawlers are visiting your site..." |
